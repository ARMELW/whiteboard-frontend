Issue Description

Problem: Exported images were shifted upward compared to what was displayed in the editor.
Root Cause:
  • Editor (SceneCanvas.jsx): Images positioned with top-left corner at (x, y)
  • Export (sceneExporter.js, etc.): Images positioned with center at (x, y)
This mismatch caused images to appear in different positions between editor and export.
